The tower is 324 metres (1,063 ft) tall, about the same height as an 81-storey building . 
During its construction, the Eiffel Tower surpassed Washington Monument to become the tallest man-made structure in the world . 
Due to the addition of a broadcasting aerial at the top of the tower, it is now taller than the Chrysler Building by 5.2 metres (17 ft) .
